Overview

Siri Kuvi Investment Ltd (SKIL) is a proudly Papua New Guinean-owned company committed to transforming the way communities are built and experienced. Located on Portion 2305 in Port Moresby, Siri Kuvi Estate represents the next evolution of urban living — a carefully planned environment where residential comfort, commercial opportunity, and community wellbeing exist in one integrated ecosystem. This is more than a development. It is a long-term vision to create sustainable, accessible, and high-quality living spaces for hardworking Papua New Guineans.

The Vision

"Beyond Tomorrow"

A balanced ecosystem of:

  • Residential living
  • Commercial opportunity
  • Community spaces

Built to raise the standard of living in Port Moresby.
