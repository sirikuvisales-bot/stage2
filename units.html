Overview

This page outlines the proposed planning, design, and construction of a 2 x 10-unit residential complex within 2G Estate. This project represents a long-term development initiative aimed at delivering a structured, high-quality housing solution within the estate. Unlike the other works currently underway, this project is positioned as a strategic development phase, with emphasis placed on proper planning, cost control, and staged execution.

Project Scope

1. Planning & Concept Development

Development of the overall concept for the site, including layout configuration, unit arrangement, and efficient use of available land.

Objective:

  • Maximize land utilization
  • Establish a functional and marketable housing layout

2. Architectural & Structural Design

Preparation of detailed drawings and design documentation covering unit layouts, structural systems, and building specifications.

Objective:

  • Ensure compliance with construction standards
  • Deliver practical and efficient unit designs

3. Infrastructure & Services Planning

Integration of essential services including access roads, drainage systems, utilities, and general site infrastructure.

Objective:

  • Support long-term sustainability of the development
  • Ensure proper servicing of all units

4. Costing & Development Strategy

Preparation of a comprehensive Bill of Quantities (BOQ), cost estimates, and a phased construction plan.

Objective:

  • Provide financial clarity
  • Enable structured and controlled project execution

Current Status

The project is currently in the planning and design stage, running in parallel with the execution of higher-priority construction works.

Next Steps

- Finalize concept design and layout
- Develop detailed drawings and BOQ
- Establish phased construction timeline
- Prepare for implementation upon approval
