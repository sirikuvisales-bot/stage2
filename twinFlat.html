Overview

This page outlines the scope of works required to bring the twin-flat complex to a fully functional and tenant-ready standard. While the completion of the verandah forms a key component of this project, it is part of a broader set of works necessary to support comfortable and practical occupancy. With both tenants already in place, this project has been elevated to priority status, with a focus on completing essential infrastructure and access improvements in a timely and efficient manner.

1. Verandah Completion

This entails the finalization of the existing verandah structure, railing, roofing, access staircase and ceiling works.

Payment Options

To provide flexibility and convenience, two financial pathways are available for the completion of the verandah works.

Option 1: Full Upfront Payment

This option allows for a single full payment prior to commencement of works.

Total - K22,740.30

Benefit:

  • 15% reduction in labour costs
  • Enables concurrent execution of multiple work stages
  • Shorter overall project duration
  • Simplified financial arrangement (single payment)

Option 2: Staged Payments

This option follows a structured payment plan aligned with the progression of works.

Phase 6 - Electrical wiring, lighting and GPO: K810.00

Phase 7 - Ceiling works including metal stud ceiling frames and Plasterboards: K10,160.00

Note:
