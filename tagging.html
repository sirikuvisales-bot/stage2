Overview

This page outlines the proposed land tagging works for the estate, aimed at clearly identifying and marking individual property boundaries in accordance with the predefined demarcation plan. This process is essential in preparing the land for orderly development, allocation, and future construction activities. The works are structured to ensure accuracy, consistency, and efficiency across the entire site.

Project Scope

1. Demarcation Plan Review

Assessment and interpretation of the existing layout and boundary plan to guide accurate positioning on site.

Objective:

  • Ensure alignment with the approved estate layout
  • Establish clear reference points for execution

2. Boundary Identification & Verification

On-site confirmation of plot boundaries using the demarcation plan as a guide.

Objective:

  • Accurately translate plan dimensions onto the ground
  • Minimize discrepancies during marking

3. Pegging & Physical Marking

Installation of physical markers to define individual plot boundaries across the estate.

Objective:

  • Clearly distinguish each parcel of land
  • Provide visible and practical boundary indicators

4. Tagging & Labeling

Assignment and placement of identifiers to each marked plot for easy reference and organization.

Objective:

  • Improve traceability and management of plots
  • Support future allocation and development processes
